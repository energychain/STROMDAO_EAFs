Metering.updateReading

  • meterId : String
    A unique identifier for the meter whose reading is being updated. This is often in the form of a MeLoId or a similar standardized identifier.
  • time : BigDecimal
    The UNIX timestamp, in milliseconds, indicating when the meter reading was taken, based on UTC. If this parameter is omitted, the system's current time will be used by default.
  • reading : BigDecimal
    The actual energy consumption measurement from the meter, recorded in watt-hours (Wh) at the time specified by the `time` parameter.
